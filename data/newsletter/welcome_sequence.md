# CampIn Welcome Sequence

## Email 1: Welcome To CampIn

Send:

Immediately.

Subject:

Welcome to CampIn

Goal:

Set expectations and explain that CampIn is validating the trust layer before opening bookings.

CTA:

Reply with the first place you want CampIn to verify.

## Email 2: What Makes Camping Safe?

Send:

2 days after signup.

Subject:

What would make camping in India feel safe enough?

Goal:

Collect top safety blockers.

CTA:

Complete safety survey.

## Email 3: Own Tent, Hosted Tent, Or Road Stop?

Send:

5 days after signup.

Subject:

Which kind of camper are you?

Goal:

Segment users into own-gear, hosted tent, car camping, campervan, family, or trekker.

CTA:

Choose your camping type.

## Email 4: Help Map The First 10 CampIn Spots

Send:

10 days after signup.

Subject:

Know a farm, estate, or road stop?

Goal:

Collect host and road-stop referrals.

CTA:

Suggest a host or road stop.
