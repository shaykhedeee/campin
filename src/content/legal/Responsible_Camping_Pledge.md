# CampIn Responsible Camping Pledge

Draft version: 2026-05-21

Every CampIn camper should accept this pledge before joining pilots, meetups, or verified community trips.

## I Pledge To

- Camp only where permission is clear.
- Respect hosts, local residents, farms, forests, water bodies, and wildlife.
- Leave no trash behind.
- Avoid loud music, nuisance, or behavior that damages future access for other campers.
- Use toilets and waste systems responsibly.
- Follow fire rules and avoid campfires unless explicitly permitted.
- Carry basic personal safety, medication, water, lights, and weather protection.
- Avoid alcohol/drug misuse that creates safety issues.
- Follow arrival, parking, quiet-hour, and departure instructions.
- Report safety or amenity mismatches honestly.
- Treat CampIn as a trust community, not a loophole for trespassing.

## CampIn's Promise Back

CampIn will work to:

- Mark verified and unverified claims clearly.
- Prioritize washroom, water, access, permission, and emergency contact visibility.
- Listen to safety concerns.
- Avoid pushing users into unsafe or unclear sites.
- Improve the camping ecosystem for hosts, campers, and local communities.
