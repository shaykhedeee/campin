# CampIn Privacy Notice

Draft version: 2026-05-21

This Privacy Notice explains how CampIn collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data during its validation, community, host onboarding, newsletter, and pilot phases.

## 1. Data We Collect

Camper waitlist:

- Name
- Email
- City
- Camping type
- Own-gear status
- Preferred region
- Safety concern
- WhatsApp opt-in
- Newsletter consent

Host interest:

- Name/contact person
- Phone/email
- Property type
- Location pin
- Amenities
- Photos
- Permission status
- Expected price
- Availability and access notes

Road-stop leads:

- Route
- Location pin
- Local contact
- Parking capacity
- Washroom/water/power availability
- Vehicle access
- Night safety notes

Community:

- Name or display name
- Phone number if joining WhatsApp
- City/region interest
- Community participation notes where voluntarily shared

## 2. Why We Use Data

CampIn uses data to:

- Build and operate the waitlist.
- Send The Campfire newsletter where consented.
- Invite users to WhatsApp communities where opted in.
- Understand camping demand by city, region, and safety concern.
- Qualify hosts and road-stop candidates.
- Coordinate manual pilot bookings.
- Improve safety, content, SEO/AEO pages, and support.
- Prevent spam, misuse, and unsafe activity.

## 3. Consent

Newsletter consent and WhatsApp opt-in must be separate from general form submission.

Users should be able to:

- Join waitlist without joining WhatsApp.
- Join newsletter only if they opt in.
- Unsubscribe from newsletter.
- Leave WhatsApp community at any time.
- Request correction or deletion of their personal data.

## 4. Research Boundaries

CampIn may study public posts, forums, search results, competitor pages, and official sources to understand the camping ecosystem.

CampIn must not:

- Scrape private groups.
- Collect Reddit usernames or personal identifiers.
- Build lead lists from personal social profiles without consent.
- Mass-message users from communities.
- Bypass robots.txt, API rules, or platform terms.

## 5. Sharing

CampIn may share necessary booking or pilot coordination details with relevant hosts or service providers only when needed.

CampIn may use trusted tools such as Tally, Airtable, Brevo, Google Workspace, analytics tools, or payment providers. These tools may process data according to their own terms.

CampIn should not sell personal data.

## 6. Retention

Suggested retention:

- Waitlist leads: until user unsubscribes/deletion request or 24 months of inactivity.
- Host applications: 36 months or until rejected and no longer needed.
- Pilot booking records: as needed for accounting, dispute, and safety purposes.
- Community incident records: as needed to protect users and enforce rules.

## 7. Security

CampIn should use:

- Restricted access to Airtable/Sheets.
- Strong passwords and two-factor authentication.
- Minimal public sharing of forms and databases.
- Separate public content from private lead records.
- Regular export/backups for operational records.

## 8. User Rights

Users may contact CampIn to request:

- Access to their data.
- Correction.
- Withdrawal of consent.
- Deletion where legally and operationally feasible.
- Unsubscribe from communication.
