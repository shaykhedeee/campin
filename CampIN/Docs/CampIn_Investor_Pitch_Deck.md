## SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM

### India's $86 Billion Outdoor Travel Market Has No Platform

**Three unsolved problems:**

1. **Discovery is broken**
   - Campers can't find verified, safe camping spots online
   - No organized directory of farm stays, forest camps, or terrace camping
   - Google searches lead to outdated blogs or overpriced glamping resorts

2. **Supply is invisible**
   - Thousands of beautiful land parcels (farms, terraces, forests) sit empty
   - Landowners have no way to reach camping enthusiasts
   - No trust framework exists for strangers camping on private land

3. **Road travelers are stranded**
   - Campervan/vanlife culture is exploding in India
   - NO organized network of safe overnight stops exists
   - Travelers park at dhabas or temples — unsafe, no amenities

**Proof of problem:** CampMonk (India's closest competitor) earns ₹2.55Cr/year with a limited, South India-only, non-platform approach. Imagine what a proper national platform could do.

---

## SLIDE 3: THE SOLUTION

### CampIn — India's Hipcamp

A **two-sided marketplace** connecting landowners (hosts) with outdoor enthusiasts (campers) across India.

**Three unique pillars:**
1. 🏕️ **Camping Marketplace** — Farms, forests, terraces, desert, lakeside stays
2. 🚐 **Road Stop Network** — India's first verified campervan/road-trip stop directory
3. 💛 **Community Engine** — WhatsApp groups, Instagram content, camping meetups, route guides

**How it works:**
```
Host lists space (free) → Camper discovers & books → CampIn takes 10% + 5-8% service fee
```

| Premium Membership | ₹499/month for campers | 90%+ | Month 24+ |

**Unit Economics (per booking):**
```
Average booking: ₹2,250 (1.5 nights @ ₹1,500/night)
Host commission (10%): ₹225
Service fee (7%): ₹157
Total revenue per booking: ₹382
Payment processing: -₹45
Net revenue per booking: ₹337
```

**Break-even: 65 bookings/month at ₹25K monthly costs**
