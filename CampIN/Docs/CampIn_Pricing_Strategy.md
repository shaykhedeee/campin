# 💲 CAMPIN — PRICING STRATEGY
> How CampIn prices everything — platform fees, host commissions, premium tiers, and partner deals

---

## 1. PRICING PHILOSOPHY

1. **Accessible, not luxury** — Camping should be cheaper than hotels
2. **Host-friendly** — Free to list, keep 90%, no hidden fees
3. **Transparent** — Every cost visible before booking
4. **India-first** — Prices in ₹, UPI-friendly, wallet-compatible
5. **Phase-appropriate** — Don't monetize before proving value

---

## 2. CAMPER PRICING (What Campers Pay)

### Listing Price Range
| Category | Min | Avg | Max | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Basic tent camping | ₹400 | ₹800 | ₹1,500 | Just space + basic amenities |
| Farm stay | ₹800 | ₹1,500 | ₹3,000 | Working farm, local food |
| Forest campsite | ₹600 | ₹1,200 | ₹2,500 | Private forest access |
| Terrace camping | ₹800 | ₹1,500 | ₹3,000 | Scenic views, often unique |
| Road Stop (basic) | ₹200 | ₹400 | ₹700 | Safe parking + water + washroom |
| Road Stop (premium) | ₹500 | ₹800 | ₹1,500 | + Electricity + food + security |
| Desert camping | ₹800 | ₹1,500 | ₹3,000 | Often includes cultural experience |
| Lakeside / Riverside | ₹600 | ₹1,200 | ₹2,500 | Water access, views |

### Service Fee (Paid by Camper)
- **5-8% of booking total**
- Displayed separately at checkout
- Covers: Payment processing, platform maintenance, customer support
- **Example:** ₹1,500/night × 2 nights = ₹3,000 → Service fee: ₹180 (6%)

### Cancellation Policy (Campers)
| Timing | Refund |
|---|---|
| 7+ days before check-in | 100% refund (minus service fee) |
| 3-7 days before check-in | 50% refund |
| < 3 days before check-in | No refund |
| Host cancels | 100% refund + ₹500 credit |

## 3. HOST PRICING (What Hosts Pay)

### Platform Commission
- **10% of each booking** (one of the lowest in Indian travel)
- Includes: Payment processing, platform maintenance, discovery, support
- Host receives 90% directly to bank account

**Example:**
```
Booking: ₹1,500/night × 2 nights = ₹3,000
Camper service fee (6%): ₹180 (paid by camper)
Host receives: ₹2,700 (90%)
CampIn revenue: ₹300 (10%) + ₹180 (service fee) = ₹480
```

### Listing Tiers (Phase 2)

| Tier | Cost | Benefits |
|---|---|---|
| **Basic** | Free | Standard listing, appears in search results |
| **Featured** | ₹500-2,000/month | Priority placement, "Featured" badge, appears in top 10 |
| **Verified Host** | ₹999/year | "CampIn Verified" badge, priority in search, dedicated support |
| **Premium** | ₹2,999/year | All above + professional photography + listing optimization + analytics |

### No Hidden Fees
- No setup fee
- No monthly fee (unless choosing Featured tier)
- No contract
- No exclusivity requirement
- No listing renewal fee

## 8. PRICING EXPERIMENTS TO RUN

### Experiment 1: Host Commission (Month 7-9)
Test 8% vs 10% vs 12% commission. Measure:
- Host signup rate at each tier
- Host retention at each tier
- Revenue per host at each tier
- **Decision:** Choose the rate that maximizes total platform revenue (not per-booking revenue)

### Experiment 2: Camper Service Fee (Month 9-12)
Test 5% vs 8% vs 10% service fee. Measure:
- Booking conversion rate at each tier
- Camper repeat rate at each tier
- **Decision:** Choose the rate that maximizes total bookings × fee

### Experiment 3: Free vs Paid Featured Listings (Month 12+)
Test free featured (random rotation) vs paid featured. Measure:
- Host willingness to pay
- Incremental bookings for featured listings
- **Decision:** Launch paid featured only if hosts see 3X+ ROI
