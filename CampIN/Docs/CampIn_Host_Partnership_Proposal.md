### Top Amenities That Drive Bookings

| Amenity | Impact on Bookings |
|---|---|
| ✅ Clean washroom access | HIGH — #1 requested amenity in India |
| ✅ Drinking water | HIGH — Essential for all campers |
| ✅ Parking space | MEDIUM — Important for car campers |
| ✅ Campfire area | MEDIUM — Top experience driver |

## 9. FAQ

**Q: Do I need to have tents or camping equipment?**
A: No! Campers bring their own tents and gear. You just provide the space and basic amenities.

**Q: What if I don't have a washroom?**
A: Many campers are happy with basic facilities. We'll set expectations clearly on your listing.

**Q: Can I list on CampIn AND other platforms?**
A: Absolutely. No exclusivity required. We believe the more exposure, the better.

**Q: Who sets the price?**
A: You do. We'll recommend a range based on your area and amenities, but the final price is always yours.

**Q: What if a camper damages my property?**
A: Contact us immediately. We mediate disputes and will have insurance coverage (Phase 2).

**Q: How many bookings should I expect?**
A: This varies widely. Scenic properties near popular routes can get 10–30 bookings/month. Remote locations may get 2–5/month.

**Q: Can I pause or deactivate my listing?**
A: Yes, anytime. Just update your availability calendar.
