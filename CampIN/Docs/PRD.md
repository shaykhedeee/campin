# CampIn — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Vision
**CampIn** is India's category-defining platform for outdoor stays, focusing on private land (farms, terraces, forests) and dedicated campervan infrastructure. It is the "Airbnb for Camping in India."

## Core Target Audience
1. **Campers:** Millennials/Gen-Z looking for offbeat nature stays.
2. **Road Trippers:** Vanlifers and overlanders needing safe overnight stops with basic amenities (water, washroom, electricity).
3. **Hosts:** Landowners (farms, tea estates, forest-edge properties) looking to monetize their space.

## MVP Scope (Phase 1)
The primary goal is **Trust and Lead Generation**.
- **Discovery:** High-quality listing cards for dummy and initial verified properties.
- **Lead Capture:** Forms for "Listing Your Space" and "User Signups."
- **Community Support:** A "Support CampIn" page to build community backing.
- **Emotional Branding:** Premium, nature-focused design that feels "Indian" and "Human."

## Key Pages
- **Home:** Emotional hook, categories (Terrace, Farm, Forest, Campervan), and community mission.
- **Explore:** Filterable search for campsites and road stops.
- **Signup:** Simple lead capture for campers.
- **List Your Space:** Comprehensive form for prospective hosts.
- **Support:** Community funding page.

## Unique Selling Point (USP)
The **Road Stop** category: Specifically verified locations with washrooms, water, and electricity for people traveling in their own vehicles (SUV camping, Campervans, Motorbikes).
