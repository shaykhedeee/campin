# CampIn — Design System

## Brand Philosophy
- **Keywords:** Calm, Warm, Expansive, Real.
- **Inspiration:** Airbnb (Simplicity), Patagonia (Storytelling), Notion (Cleanliness).

## Color Palette
- **Forest Green (`#1F3D2B`):** Primary brand color. Used for headers, buttons, and trust elements.
- **Warm Orange (`#E67E22`):** Accent color. Used for active states, notifications, and "Book" CTAs.
- **Off-White (`#F7F5F2`):** Main background color to keep the site feeling warm and light.
- **Text Grey (`#5F5F5F`):** Secondary text color for better readability and a softer feel.

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (Sans-serif)
- **H1:** 56–72px, Bold, Forest Green.
- **Body:** 16–18px, Regular, 1.7 line height.

## Component Rules
- **Radius:** 16px (Rounded corners) for cards and buttons to feel friendly.
- **Shadows:** Soft, subtle elevation (`0 4px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`).
- **Cards:** 60% image height, object-cover, soft transition on hover.
