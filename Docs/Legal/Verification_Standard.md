# CampIn Verification Standard

Draft version: 2026-05-21

CampIn verification exists to reduce camping guesswork. It is not a guarantee of zero risk.

## Verification Labels

| Label | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Unverified | CampIn has not reviewed the claim |
| Host-declared | Host has provided the information, not independently confirmed |
| Community-reported | A camper reported it, but CampIn has not verified |
| Founder-call reviewed | CampIn has spoken to the host/contact |
| Photo reviewed | Photos show the claimed amenity or access |
| CampIn Reviewed | CampIn has completed the minimum review checklist |
| Road Stop Standard | Road-stop candidate meets minimum overnight stop checklist |
| Pilot-ready | CampIn believes the site can be used for a controlled manual pilot |

## CampIn Reviewed Minimum Checklist

- Exact location pin collected.
- Host/contact identity logged.
- Permission or operating status documented.
- Washroom status documented.
- Water status documented.
- Parking/vehicle access documented.
- Arrival instructions documented.
- Emergency contact documented.
- Restrictions documented.
- Price and extra charges documented.
- Photos reviewed.
- Safety concerns noted.

## Road Stop Standard Minimum Checklist

- Route identified.
- Parking capacity estimated.
- Vehicle access confirmed for relevant vehicle types.
- Washroom available or clearly marked unavailable.
- Water available or clearly marked unavailable.
- Power availability marked.
- Night safety assessed by host/contact and/or community report.
- Local contact identified.
- Permission for overnight stop discussed.

## Evidence Types

- Host form.
- Founder call notes.
- Photos.
- Video walkthrough.
- Location pin.
- Public business page.
- Government or tourism reference where relevant.
- Camper pilot feedback.
- On-site visit.

## Claims That Must Never Be Assumed

- Legal camping permission.
- Campfire permission.
- Forest/beach/riverbank access.
- Safety for solo/female campers.
- 24-hour access.
- Pet permission.
- Alcohol permission.
- Refund eligibility.

## Review Cadence

- Host-declared: refresh every 90 days.
- CampIn Reviewed: refresh every 180 days or after any incident.
- Road Stop Standard: refresh every 90 days for first year.
- Pilot-ready: re-check before each manual pilot.
