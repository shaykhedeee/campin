# CampIn Legal Document System

Last updated: 2026-05-21

Important:

These are founder-ready policy drafts, not legal advice. Before accepting payments, publishing live listings, or operating as a marketplace, CampIn should have an Indian lawyer review the final documents.

## Required Public Legal Pages

| Document | URL | Required Before | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Terms Of Service | `/terms` | Public launch | Governs use of CampIn |
| Privacy Notice | `/privacy` | Any lead capture | Explains personal data collection, purpose, consent, retention, rights |
| Cancellation And Refund Policy | `/cancellation-refund-policy` | Paid pilot | Sets booking, cancellation, refund, and weather rules |
| Community Guidelines | `/community-guidelines` | WhatsApp community | Moderation and member conduct |
| Responsible Camping Pledge | `/responsible-camping-pledge` | Community and pilots | Camper behavior standard |
| Host Agreement | `/host-agreement` | Host onboarding | Host obligations and verification claims |
| Verification Standard | `/verification-standard` | Trust badges | Defines CampIn Reviewed and Road Stop Standard |
| Grievance Redressal | `/grievance-redressal` | Marketplace/payment phase | Consumer complaint channel |
| Cookie Policy | `/cookie-policy` | Analytics/ads phase | Explains analytics and tracking |

## Compliance Principles

- Collect only data needed for validation, community, host onboarding, safety, and communication.
- Use explicit consent for newsletter and WhatsApp opt-in.
- Do not collect private community data or personal data from Reddit/forums.
- Do not imply a site is verified unless the evidence exists.
- Keep cancellation/refund terms visible before taking payment.
- Keep a contact and grievance channel visible once bookings begin.
- Mark CampIn's role clearly: pre-booking validation platform now, marketplace later.

## Legal Risk Register

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| User assumes unverified place is safe/legal | High | High | Verified/host-declared/unknown labels |
| Host misstates permission | Medium | High | Host agreement, proof checklist, verification stage |
| Safety incident during pilot | Medium | High | Emergency contacts, incident SOP, disclaimers, insurance exploration |
| Refund dispute | Medium | Medium | Plain cancellation policy before payment |
| Spam or privacy complaint | Medium | Medium | Consent logs, unsubscribe, no scraped personal data |
| Community misuse | Medium | Medium | Guidelines, moderation, reporting |
| Wildfire/campfire issue | Low/Medium | High | Fire allowed only when explicitly permitted |

## Review Gate

Before any paid pilot:

- Terms reviewed.
- Privacy notice public.
- Cancellation/refund policy public.
- Responsible Camping Pledge accepted by camper.
- Host Agreement accepted by host.
- Site verification status documented.
- Emergency contact documented.

Before marketplace launch:

- Lawyer review complete.
- Grievance officer/contact workflow ready.
- Payment/refund flow documented.
- Incident response workflow tested.
- Insurance and liability posture reviewed.
