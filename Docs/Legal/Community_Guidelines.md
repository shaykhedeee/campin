# CampIn Community Guidelines

Draft version: 2026-05-21

CampIn's community exists to make camping in India safer, clearer, more respectful, and more fun.

## 1. Core Rules

- Be respectful.
- Do not spam.
- Do not share illegal or unsafe camping instructions.
- Do not reveal private land, private contact details, or exact sensitive locations without consent.
- Mark unverified information as unverified.
- Do not promote trips, stays, or services without moderator permission.
- Do not harass, discriminate, threaten, or shame members.
- Do not encourage littering, trespassing, reckless driving, unsafe fires, or wildlife disturbance.

## 2. Location Sharing

Members may share general regions, route tips, and public information. Exact pins for private land, sensitive natural areas, or unverified campsites should not be shared publicly without permission.

## 3. Safety Discussions

Safety questions are welcome. Claims about permissions, forest access, local restrictions, campfire rules, or night safety must be framed carefully:

- Verified: evidence exists.
- Host-declared: host has claimed it.
- Community-reported: a member experienced it, but CampIn has not verified.
- Unknown: do not assume.

## 4. Promotions

Hosts, guides, gear sellers, creators, and trip operators may not repeatedly promote services inside the community. CampIn may create approved threads or forms for relevant opportunities.

## 5. Moderation

CampIn may remove posts, warn members, mute members, or remove members to protect the community.

Serious issues such as harassment, fraud, unsafe advice, illegal activity, or doxxing can lead to immediate removal.

## 6. Reporting

Report concerns to:

hello@campin.co.in

Include screenshots, context, date, and the group where the issue occurred when possible.
