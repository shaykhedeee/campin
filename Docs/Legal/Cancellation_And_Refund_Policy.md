# CampIn Cancellation And Refund Policy

Draft version: 2026-05-21

This policy applies to manually coordinated CampIn pilot stays and road-stop pilots. It should be shown before any payment is collected.

## 1. Validation Phase

During validation, most CampIn activity is waitlist, research, community, and host onboarding. A user is not charged unless a manual pilot is explicitly confirmed.

## 2. Confirmed Pilot

A pilot is confirmed only when CampIn or the host confirms:

- Site
- Date
- Number of guests/vehicles
- Price
- Included amenities
- Arrival window
- Cancellation terms
- Responsible Camping Pledge acceptance

## 3. Camper Cancellation

Recommended default:

- More than 72 hours before arrival: full refund minus payment gateway charges, if any.
- 24 to 72 hours before arrival: 50% refund unless host-specific terms say otherwise.
- Less than 24 hours before arrival: no refund unless CampIn or host approves an exception.

## 4. Host Or CampIn Cancellation

If a host or CampIn cancels because of site unavailability, permission issue, safety concern, severe weather, or operational failure, CampIn should offer:

- Full refund, or
- Alternative date/site if user accepts.

## 5. Weather And Safety

Outdoor stays can be affected by rain, heat, road conditions, local restrictions, wildlife, or emergency events. CampIn may cancel or reschedule if continuing creates unreasonable safety risk.

## 6. No-Show

If a camper does not arrive within the agreed arrival window and does not contact CampIn/host, the pilot may be treated as a no-show and may not be refundable.

## 7. Amenity Mismatch

If a materially promised amenity is unavailable, such as washroom, water, parking, or access, CampIn should investigate and may offer a partial or full refund depending on severity.

## 8. Damage, Misconduct, And Rule Violations

Refunds may be denied if the camper violates rules, damages property, harasses people, trespasses, starts unauthorized fires, litters, or ignores safety instructions.

## 9. Refund Timing

Refunds should be processed through the original payment method where possible. Payment provider timelines may vary.
