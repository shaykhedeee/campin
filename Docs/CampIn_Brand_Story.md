## THE ORIGIN STORY

It started with a simple frustration.

India has some of the most beautiful landscapes on Earth — the misty terraces of Meghalaya, the coffee-scented hills of Coorg, the ancient cedar forests of Himachal, the silent deserts of Jaisalmer, the apple orchards of Kashmir. Yet if you wanted to sleep under the stars in any of these places, your options were:

1. An overpriced "glamping resort" that costs more than a hotel
2. An unverified listing on a generic travel site with no reviews
3. Pitching a tent somewhere and hoping you don't get asked to leave

There was no platform. No community. No trusted way to discover, book, and experience camping in India.

Meanwhile, halfway across the world, a platform called Hipcamp had already solved this problem for America. Seven million campers. 140,000 campsites. A booking every 30 seconds. Landowners earning $150,000/year from simply welcoming campers onto their property.

The model was proven. The technology existed. But nobody had built it for India.

That's where CampIn comes in.

**CampIn is India's answer to Hipcamp — a community-first camping platform that connects landowners with outdoor enthusiasts across the country.**

Not a booking website. Not a travel agency. A movement to build India's outdoor culture.

## THE BELIEF

We believe something fundamental:

**The best experiences in India happen outdoors.**

Not in hotel rooms. Not in conference halls. Not scrolling through Instagram.

They happen around campfires in Coorg, where the coffee smell mixes with woodsmoke. They happen on terraces in Meghalaya, where you wake up above the clouds. They happen on mountain roads in Spiti, where your campervan becomes your home for the night.

**CampIn exists to make these experiences accessible to every Indian.**

Not just wealthy travelers. Not just Instagram-famous adventurers. Everyone. The college student who's never camped before. The family looking for a weekend that doesn't involve a mall. The vanlife couple who needs a safe place to park for the night. The farm owner who wants to share their land and earn from it.

## THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

### For Campers
You want to go camping. But where? Google searches give you outdated blog posts. Travel apps give you luxury resorts pretending to be "camps." There's no single place where you can search for a forest campsite in Himachal, filter by "has washroom" and "allows campfires," read reviews from real campers, and book it — all in two minutes.

CampIn is that place.

### For Hosts
You own a beautiful piece of land — a farm, a forest patch, a hillside terrace. It sits there, doing nothing, while you pay property tax on it. What if you could open it to campers? What if you could earn ₹50,000, ₹2 lakh, even ₹8 lakh a year — just by letting people pitch tents on your property?

But you don't know how to start. You don't know how to reach campers. You're worried about safety. You don't want to deal with payments and bookings.

CampIn handles all of it. For free.

### For Road Travelers
You drive a campervan, an SUV, or a bike. You love the freedom of the road. But every evening, the same question: "Where do we sleep tonight?" There's no network of safe overnight stops in India. No directory of places with water, washrooms, electricity. You park at dhabas, behind temples, on the side of the road — and you never fully relax.

CampIn is building India's first verified road stop network. Safe. Verified. Amenities confirmed. Community-rated.

## THE THREE PILLARS

### 🏕️ The Marketplace
A two-sided platform where hosts list spaces for free, campers discover and book them, and CampIn takes a small commission. Simple. Proven globally by Hipcamp and Airbnb. Adapted for India.

### 🚐 The Road Stop Network
India's first dedicated directory of safe overnight stops for campervans, SUVs, and road travelers. Verified parking, water, washrooms, electricity. Built route by route on major corridors.

### 💛 The Community Engine
WhatsApp groups by region. Instagram storytelling. Monthly camping meetups. Camping guides and route planners. Host stories. Camper testimonials. A living, breathing community — not just a transaction platform.
