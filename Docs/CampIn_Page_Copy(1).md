## HOST YOUR LAND PAGE

- **H1:** Host your land on CampIn
- **Body:** Join the movement to open up India's most beautiful private spaces. Every Indian has a campfire story in their soul; help them write it on your land.

### Why Host
- Free to List: No monthly fees. No strings.
- Keep 92%: We take a minimal 8% to fuel the movement. You keep the rest.
- Manual Verification: We protect your land by reviewing every camper and every listing personally.
- Amenity Boost: Add as many amenities as you can (Washrooms, Fire Places, Guides) to increase your earning potential.

### Form Sections
- Contact Information: Full Name, Phone, Email
- Exact Location: **Google Maps Pin Link (Verified)**
- Real Property Pictures: (Actual photos of the site campers will use are mandatory)
- Property Type: Farm, Terrace, Forest, Lakeside, Desert, Orchard, Roadside, Other
- Amenities: Fire Place (NEW), Washroom (NEW), Drinking Water, Electricity, Parking, Local Food, Mobile Signal, Pet Friendly, Guides
- Description: "Describe your space — get to the heart of the desire for disconnection."
- Photos: "Upload 5-10 actual location pictures. Must include the exact site users will use."
- Button: "Submit for Backend Review"
- Trust: "Listing does not go live immediately. Our team reviews every submission for safety, beauty, and accuracy before acceptance."
