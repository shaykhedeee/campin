## 1. WHAT IS CAMPIN?

CampIn is **India's outdoor identity in digital form**. It is a community-first, two-sided marketplace platform connecting landowners (hosts) with outdoor enthusiasts (campers) across India.

**Mission:** When an Indian says "I want to go camping," CampIn.co.in is the first and only name that comes to mind.

**Tagline:** Stay. Explore. CampIn.
**Hero Line:** "Wake up to views, not hotel bills."

CampIn is NOT:
- Just a booking website
- A glamping-only platform
- A luxury travel aggregator
- A content blog

CampIn IS:
- A category-defining platform for outdoor stays in India
- A community builder for campers, vanlifers, road trippers, and nature lovers
- India's first dedicated campervan stop directory
- A trust layer between hosts and campers
- A movement to make India's outdoors accessible to every Indian

## 2. MARKET OPPORTUNITY

### The Numbers
- India Camping Equipment Market: USD 892M (2024) → USD 2,027M (2030)
- CAGR: 14.66% (2025–2030)
- Camping experiences/stays market is layered on top of equipment
- Platform layer is almost entirely unorganized in India
- Post-COVID spike in outdoor/adventure travel
- Rising millennial/Gen-Z interest, social media influence, remote work culture

### The Van Life Opportunity
- Campervan culture growing rapidly in India
- Remote work enabling nomadic lifestyles
- Major gap: NO organized platform for safe overnight stops
- Campervan owners need: water, washrooms, electricity, safe parking, trusted hosts

### Government Support
- Swadesh Darshan Scheme
- Growing adventure travel infrastructure
- State tourism board openness to private partnerships

## 4. BUSINESS MODEL

### Core: Two-Sided Marketplace
```
HOST → Lists space for free → Sets price + amenities → Manages bookings
CAMPIN → Discovery + trust layer → 10% from host + 5-8% service fee from camper
CAMPER → Discovers spaces → Books directly → Reviews + builds community
```

### Revenue Streams (Phase by Phase)

**Phase 1 (Months 1–6): VALIDATION — NO REVENUE FOCUS**
- Lead generation only, host onboarding (free), user signups, community building
- Revenue Goal: ₹0. Trust Goal: Everything.

**Phase 2 (Months 7–18): EARLY MONETIZATION**
| Stream | Model | Expected |
|---|---|---|
| Booking commission (host) | 10% per booking | Primary |
| Service fee (camper) | 5–8% per booking | Secondary |
| Featured listings | ₹500–₹2000/month | Early upsell |
| Verified Host Badge | ₹999/year | Trust upsell |

**Phase 3 (Months 18–36+): SCALE**
- Affiliate gear sales (Decathlon, Wildcraft)
- Camping rental partnerships
- Guided experience bookings
- Brand collaborations (Royal Enfield, Mahindra)
- Premium memberships (₹499/month)
- Trip planning tool
- Campervan infrastructure network

### Unit Economics
```
Average stay: ₹1,500/night
CampIn commission (10% host): ₹150
CampIn service fee (7% camper): ₹105
Revenue per booking: ~₹255

100 bookings/month → ₹25,500/month
1,000 bookings/month → ₹2,55,000/month
10,000 bookings/month → ₹25,50,000/month
```
