# 🤝 CAMPIN — HOST OUTREACH SCRIPTS

> DM templates, email scripts, and phone call guides for acquiring hosts

---

## 1. OUTREACH PHILOSOPHY

- **Personal, not automated** — Every DM should feel like it was written specifically for this person
- **Value first** — Lead with what CampIn offers the host, not what we need
- **Respectful** — No pressure, no follow-up guilt trips
- **Authentic** — Sound like a real person, not a brand
- **Founder-led** — In the beginning, the founder does ALL outreach personally

---

## 2. TARGET HOST PROFILES

### Where to Find Hosts
- Instagram: #farmstay #camping #terracecamping #naturestay #indiacamping
- Google: "farm stay [location]", "camping [location]", "homestay [location]"
- Facebook Groups: Farm Stay India, Camping India, Homestay India
- Airbnb: Look for nature/unique stays in target locations
- Google Maps: Search for campsites and farm stays

### Priority Locations
1. **Coorg / Kodagu, Karnataka** — Coffee estates, farm stays
2. **Himachal Pradesh** (Jibhi, Tirthan, Manali, Dharmashala) — Forest, mountain
3. **Uttarakhand** (Rishikesh, Mussoorie, Nainital) — Riverside, mountain
4. **Meghalaya** (Cherrapunji, Shillong) — Terrace, living root bridges
5. **Rajasthan** (Jaisalmer, Udaipur, Pushkar) — Desert, lakeside
6. **Kashmir** (Pahalgam, Gulmarg, Srinagar) — Orchard, mountain
7. **Kerala** (Wayanad, Munnar, Vagamon) — Farm, forest
8. **Spiti/Ladakh** — High altitude, road stops
9. **Goa** — Beach camping, forest
10. **Near metros** (Lonavala, Karjat, Chikmagalur, Yercaud) — Weekend getaways

## 3. INSTAGRAM DM SCRIPTS

### Script A: First Contact (Farm/Nature Stay)
```
Hi [Name]! 👋

I came across your [farm/stay/property] on Instagram and 
it looks absolutely beautiful. The kind of place that 
deserves to be discovered by more people.

I'm building CampIn — it's like Airbnb but specifically 
for camping in India. We help landowners like you welcome 
campers on your property.

It's completely free to list, and you set your own price. 
We're looking for founding hosts in [their region] and 
I'd love to tell you more.

Would you be open to a quick chat? Even just 10 minutes 
on WhatsApp would be great. 

No pressure at all — just wanted to reach out because 
your space genuinely looks perfect for what we're building.

— [Your Name], Founder of CampIn 🏕️
```

### Script B: Follow-Up (if no response in 3 days)
```
Hey [Name]! Just bumping this up — I know you're busy 
running your [property]. 

Totally understand if the timing isn't right, but the 
offer is always open. We're building something special 
for hosts in [region] and I think you'd be a perfect fit.

Let me know if you ever want to chat! 🙏

— [Your Name]
```

Want to jump on a quick call? Here's my number: [phone]

## 5. PHONE CALL GUIDE (15 minutes)

### Opening (2 min)
- "Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time to chat!"
- "I'm [Name], founder of CampIn — a new camping platform in India"
- "I found your [property] and thought it would be perfect for what we're building"

### Explain CampIn (3 min)
- "CampIn is like Airbnb, but specifically for camping"
- "We connect landowners with campers — farms, forests, terraces, even road stops"
- "It's free to list, and hosts keep 90% of every booking"
- "We're building India's outdoor culture, one campsite at a time"

### Learn About Their Space (5 min)
- "Tell me about your property — what makes it special?"
- "What amenities do you have? Water, washroom, electricity, parking?"
- "Have you had campers before? How was the experience?"
- "What's your biggest concern about hosting campers?"

### Address Concerns (3 min)
- Safety: "We verify every camper through phone OTP"
- Hassle: "We handle all the discovery and booking. You just welcome guests"
- Pricing: "You set the price. Typical range is ₹800–2,500/night"
- Time: "We help you set up everything. You can pause your listing anytime"

### Close (2 min)
- "Would you like to be one of our founding hosts?"
- "All I need is a few photos and basic details"
- "I can set up the listing FOR you — you just review and approve"
- "I'll send you an email with next steps right after this call"
