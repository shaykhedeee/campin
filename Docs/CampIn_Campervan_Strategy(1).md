## 5. ROAD STOP VERIFICATION CHECKLIST

### On-Site Verification (In-Person or Video Call)

- [ ] **Parking:** Is there space for 1-3 vehicles (cars/campervans)?
- [ ] **Surface:** Is the parking surface stable (not mud/sand)?
- [ ] **Water:** Is clean drinking water available?
- [ ] **Washroom:** Is there a functional washroom (even basic)?
- [ ] **Electricity:** Is power available for charging? (Optional but noted)
- [ ] **Safety:** Is the area safe at night? (Assess: lighting, fencing, nearby houses)
- [ ] **Mobile Signal:** Which networks work? (Jio, Airtel, BSNL, Vi)
- [ ] **Access:** Is the approach road passable? (Check for low bridges, narrow lanes)
- [ ] **Proximity:** Distance from highway/main road
- [ ] **Host:** Is the host reliable and responsive? (Phone call assessment)
- [ ] **Noise:** Is the area quiet enough for sleeping?
- [ ] **Food:** Is food available nearby? (Dhaba, local restaurant, host provides)
- [ ] **Photos:** Minimum 5 photos of the property
- [ ] **GPS:** Accurate coordinates verified on Google Maps
