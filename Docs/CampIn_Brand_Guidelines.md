# 🎨 CAMPIN — BRAND GUIDELINES

> The visual soul of India's camping platform

---

## 1. BRAND IDENTITY

### Name
**CampIn** — always capitalized C and I. Never "Campin" or "Camp-In" or "camp in".

### Tagline
**Stay. Explore. CampIn.**

### Mission Statement
"Building India's outdoor culture, one campsite at a time."

### Brand Personality
- **Warm** — Like a campfire conversation, not a corporate pitch
- **Authentic** — Real Indian outdoors, not Instagram-polished fantasy
- **Calm** — Nature-first, unhurried, expansive
- **Trustworthy** — Safety, verification, transparency in everything
- **Inclusive** — For everyone — not just wealthy travelers or Instagram influencers
- **Indian** — Proudly local. Celebrating what makes Indian outdoors unique

### Brand Voice

**DO:**
- Write like you're talking to a friend who loves camping
- Use warm, conversational language
- Be specific about Indian places and experiences
- Use short, impactful sentences for headlines
- Include emotional storytelling
- Reference real Indian landscapes (Coorg, Spiti, Meghalaya, Jaisalmer)
- Use camping and nature metaphors naturally

**DON'T:**
- Use corporate jargon ("leverage", "synergy", "disrupt")
- Overpromise or exaggerate
- Use stock photography language
- Be overly casual or unprofessional
- Use English-only when Hindi/regional context would land better
- Be preachy about sustainability
- Compare to competitors directly

### Words We Use
- Discover, explore, connect, camp, experience, community, outdoor
- Views, landscapes, campfires, stars, mornings, trails
- Host, camper, listing, stay, stop (for road travelers)

### Words We Avoid
- Luxury, premium (unless specifically for a tier), booking-only language
- Resort, hotel, accommodation (we're not that)
- Disrupt, revolutionize, game-changing

## 2. COLOR SYSTEM

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Forest Green** | `#1F3D2B` | Primary — headers, buttons, CTAs, nav, footer background |
| **Forest Green Light** | `#2A5239` | Hover states on buttons, secondary backgrounds |
| **Forest Green Dark** | `#152A1D` | Active states, deep emphasis |
| **Warm Orange** | `#E67E22` | Accent — highlights, tags, active filters, star ratings, key CTAs |
| **Warm Orange Light** | `#F0A04B` | Hover states for orange elements |
| **Off White** | `#F7F5F2` | Background — page backgrounds, card fills, warm base |
| **Text Grey** | `#5F5F5F` | Body text, secondary labels, descriptions |
| **Text Dark** | `#2D2D2D` | Primary body text on light backgrounds |
| **Pure White** | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, modals, input backgrounds, overlay panels |

### Color Rules
- Forest Green and Orange should NEVER be used as large background areas together
- Orange is ALWAYS the accent — never the primary
- Off White is the default page background — NOT pure white
- Text Grey for body copy — never pure black (#000)
- Forest Green text on Off White background = primary reading experience
- White text on Forest Green background = inverted/CTA experience

## 3. TYPOGRAPHY

### Font Family
**Inter** (Google Fonts — free, clean, premium feel)

### Type Scale

| Element | Size | Weight | Color | Tracking |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| H1 (Hero) | 56–72px | Bold (700) | Forest Green or White | -0.02em |
| H2 (Section) | 36–48px | Semibold (600) | Forest Green | -0.01em |
| H3 (Subsection) | 24–30px | Medium (500) | Forest Green | Normal |
| Body Large | 18–20px | Regular (400) | Text Grey | Normal |
| Body | 16px | Regular (400) | Text Grey | Normal |
| Body Small | 14px | Regular (400) | Text Grey | Normal |
| Labels | 12–14px | Medium (500) | Orange or Forest Green | 0.08em (uppercase) |
| Button Text | 16px | Bold (600) | White or Forest Green | Normal |
| Nav Links | 14px | Medium (500) | Text Grey / Forest Green | Normal |

### Line Height
- Headings: 1.1–1.2
- Body: 1.6–1.7
- Small text: 1.5

## 4. LOGO USAGE

### Primary Logo
- Icon: 🏕️ tent emoji or custom tent icon
- Wordmark: "CampIn" in Inter Bold
- Combination: 🏕️ CampIn

### Variations
- **Horizontal:** Icon + "CampIn" side by side (for nav, headers)
- **Stacked:** Icon above "CampIn" (for splash pages, social)
- **Icon Only:** Just the tent icon (for favicon, app icon)
- **Dark:** White text on dark background
- **Light:** Green text on light background

### Clear Space
- Minimum 2x the icon height on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or modify the logo
- Minimum size: 24px height for wordmark

## 5. PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTION

### What Works
- Real Indian landscapes — Coorg farms, Spiti mountains, Meghalaya terraces
- Golden hour lighting — warm, soft, inviting
- Human moments — people around campfires, waking up to views
- Authentic campsites — real tents, real fires, real people
- Campervans on Indian roads — mountain passes, highway stops
- Close-ups of nature — morning dew, campfire embers, star trails
- Host portraits — warm, genuine, welcoming

### What Doesn't Work
- Stock photography that looks staged or generic
- Over-filtered Instagram-style images
- Non-Indian landscapes (no Swiss Alps, no American RVs)
- Ultra-luxury glamping shots (we're accessible, not exclusive)
- Empty landscapes with no human context
- Dark, moody, or intimidating imagery
- Animals in distress or dangerous situations

### Image Technical Requirements
- Minimum resolution: 1200px wide for web
- Format: WebP preferred, JPG acceptable
- File size: < 200KB per image after compression
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 for heroes, 4:3 for cards, 1:1 for thumbnails
