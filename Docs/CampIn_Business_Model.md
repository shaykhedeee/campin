# 💰 CAMPIN — BUSINESS MODEL

> Revenue streams, unit economics, and financial projections

---

## 1. CORE MODEL: TWO-SIDED MARKETPLACE

```
HOST (Landowner)          CAMPIN (Platform)          CAMPER (Traveler)
├── Lists space free      ├── Discovery layer        ├── Discovers spaces
├── Sets own price        ├── Trust & verification   ├── Books directly
├── Manages availability  ├── 10% commission (host)  ├── Pays 5-8% fee
└── Welcomes campers      ├── 5-8% service (camper)  └── Reviews & community
                           └── Community building
```

**Asset-light, infinitely scalable, community-aligned.**
We don't own the land. We own the network, the trust, and the discovery.

## 5. BREAK-EVEN ANALYSIS

```
Monthly costs (Phase 2): ~₹25,000
Revenue per booking: ~₹382
Break-even: 65 bookings/month

At ₹1,500 avg/night, 1.5 nights avg stay:
65 bookings = ~₹97,500 GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)
```

**Target: Break even by Month 15–18.**

## 7. FUNDRAISING STRATEGY

### Bootstrap Phase (Month 0–12)
- Self-funded, minimal costs
- Revenue from community support (Razorpay/Patreon)
- Validate product-market fit before seeking investment

### Seed Round (Month 12–18, if needed)
- Target: ₹50L – ₹2 Cr
- Source: India-focused angels, camping/outdoor sector angels
- Use: Engineering hire, marketing, host acquisition
- Milestone: 100+ listings, 100+ monthly bookings, ₹25K+ MRR

### Series A (Month 24–36, if scaling fast)
- Target: ₹5–15 Cr
- Source: Indian VC firms, global outdoor/travel investors
- Use: Team scaling, mobile app, national expansion, partnerships
- Milestone: 500+ listings, 1000+ monthly bookings, ₹2.5L+ MRR

## 8. KEY FINANCIAL METRICS TO TRACK

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|---|---|---|
| CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) | Total marketing / New users | < ₹200 |
| LTV (Lifetime Value) | Avg revenue per user × avg lifespan | > ₹2,000 |
| LTV:CAC Ratio | LTV / CAC | > 3:1 |
| Take Rate | Platform revenue / GMV | 15–18% |
| Host Retention | Hosts active after 6 months | > 70% |
| Camper Retention | Campers who book again | > 40% |
| Payback Period | Months to recover CAC | < 6 months |
