**CampIn Launches as India's First Community-First Camping Platform**

**[City], [Date]** — CampIn (campin.co.in), India's first community-first camping platform, announced its public launch today, aiming to transform how Indians discover, book, and experience camping across the country.

The platform connects landowners — farmers, forest patch owners, terrace property holders — with a growing community of outdoor enthusiasts seeking authentic, affordable camping experiences. CampIn operates on a marketplace model similar to Hipcamp (US) and Airbnb, where hosts list their spaces for free and keep 90% of booking revenue.

"At CampIn, we believe the best experiences in India happen outdoors — on farms, in forests, beside rivers, under stars," said [Founder Name], Founder of CampIn. "India's adventure tourism market is worth $16.7 billion and growing at 17.8% annually, yet there's no dedicated platform for camping. We're building that platform — and we're building it with community at the center."

CampIn offers a unique "Road Stops" category — India's first verified directory of safe overnight stops for campervans, SUVs, and road travelers. With van life culture rapidly growing in India, this addresses a critical gap: travelers have nowhere safe to park overnight with access to basic amenities.

**Key Facts:**
- India's adventure tourism market: $16.7B (2024), projected $86B by 2033
- India's camping equipment market: $892M, growing at 14.66% CAGR
- Free to list for hosts, no contracts or monthly fees
- Phone OTP verification for all users
- UPI, cards, and wallet payments via Razorpay
- Categories: Terrace, Farm, Forest, Campervan Stops, Car Camping, Desert, Lakeside
