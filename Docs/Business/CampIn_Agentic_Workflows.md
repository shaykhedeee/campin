# CampIn Agentic Workflows

Created: 2026-05-20

These workflows turn CampIn from a static plan into a repeating operating system. They should run against local files, produce dated outputs, and avoid scraping private data or sending messages without human approval.

## Operating Rules

- Use approved APIs, public pages, source links, and robots/API-compliant collection only.
- Do not collect Reddit usernames, private data, phone numbers, emails, or personal identifiers from public communities.
- Do not mass DM campers or hosts.
- Every workflow should produce a human-readable summary and a next-action list.
- Any outreach copy generated by an agent must wait for founder approval.
- If a workflow cannot verify a claim, it should mark the claim as unverified.

## Workflow 1: Airbnb Intelligence Sweep

Cadence: Weekly, Monday 9:00 AM IST

Purpose: keep CampIn learning from Airbnb's product, trust, host, design, and marketplace moves without copying protected assets.

Inputs:

- Airbnb investor/newsroom updates.

- CampIn strategy and design docs.

Outputs:

- CampIn action list.
- "Borrow the principle, avoid the copy" section.
- Risks and warnings.

Suggested output path: `Docs/Business/CampIn_Airbnb_Intelligence_Log.md`

## Human Review Checklist

Before publishing any agent output:

- Are all factual claims sourced?
- Is anything implying legal permission without proof?
- Are personal details removed?
- Is the copy honest about manual validation stage?
- Does it strengthen CampIn's trust layer?
- Is the next action specific enough for a founder to do this week?
