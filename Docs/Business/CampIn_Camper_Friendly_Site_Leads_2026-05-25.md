# CampIn Camper-Friendly Site Leads

Research date: 2026-05-25  
Scope: India-only public web, search results, official tourism pages, public campsite pages, and public forum/search snippets.  
Client intent: BYOT, campervan, caravan, overlanding, agritourism fields, and terrace or mountain tent spots where a guest can safely stay overnight with at least water and washroom access. Electricity is treated as a strong qualifier and direct vehicle hook-up is treated as premium.

## Inclusion Standard

A site should be listed on CampIn only if it clears one of these bars:

| Tier | Meaning | Minimum bar |
|---|---|---|
| A | Caravan/RV-ready | Explicit caravan/campervan/overlanding support, safe parking or marked bays, toilets/washrooms, drinking water or refill, charging/electricity, and host or operator control. |
| B | BYOT/car-camping ready | BYOT or car camping is explicitly allowed, with parking, washrooms/toilets, drinking water, and basic safety/host presence. |
| C | Outreach lead | Public evidence suggests camping/parking potential, but CampIn must verify vehicle access, night stay permission, water, washroom, electricity, and emergency contact. |
| Reject for now | Not CampIn-ready | Wild/boondocking spot, no toilets, no water, unclear legality, local-permission-only, or normal resort/camp with no BYOT/camper signal. |

Policy benchmark: Maharashtra's caravan policy says every parking bay should have separate electricity and water connections, enough drinking/utility water, electricity backup, fencing/security/CCTV, and separate restrooms. Kerala Tourism's caravan policy pages also describe caravan parks as safe, secure zones with electricity, water, sewage and hygiene infrastructure. Use these as the validation checklist even for private farms and campsites.

## Outreach Questions For Every Lead

1. Do you allow guests to bring their own tent, campervan, rooftop tent, or caravan for an overnight stay?
2. Is the parking/camping area inside your controlled property boundary?
3. How many vehicles can stay overnight, and what is the maximum vehicle length/height?
4. Are drinking water, toilets, showers and handwash available 24/7?
5. Is electricity available for vehicle charging or only phone/laptop charging?
6. If vehicle plug-in is available, what plug type, voltage and amperage?
7. Is there any grey-water/black-water disposal rule or facility?
8. Is food available on-site or nearby after dark?
9. Is the property fenced, guarded, lit, or covered by CCTV?
10. Which mobile networks work reliably?
11. Are pets allowed?
12. Is the approach road suitable in monsoon/winter?
13. What is the nightly parking/BYOT/campervan tariff?
14. Can CampIn publish photos, GPS, host phone, rules and cancellation policy?
