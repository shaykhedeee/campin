# CampIn Outreach Operating System

Last updated: 2026-05-21

## Principle

CampIn outreach must be founder-led, low-volume, useful, and permission-respecting.

The goal is not to blast people. The goal is to find real hosts, road-stop partners, outdoor communities, and early campers who already have the problem CampIn solves.

## Daily Outreach Workflow

Daily target:

- 10 host or road-stop candidates researched.
- 5 personalized messages drafted.
- 3 founder-approved messages sent manually.
- 1 community insight logged.
- 1 objection added to the Research OS.

## Outreach Sources

Allowed:

- Public business websites.
- Public Instagram business pages.
- Google Maps business listings researched manually.
- Tourism board/business directories.
- Host applications.
- Road-stop suggestions submitted through CampIn.
- Personal referrals.
- Community admins contacted with permission.

Not allowed:

- Scraping personal Reddit users.
- Mass DMs.
- Private WhatsApp/Facebook group scraping.
- Buying email lists.
- Contacting private individuals from forum posts without consent.

## Lead Scoring

| Signal | Points |
|---|---:|
| Located in Bangalore/South India wedge | 2 |
| Has washroom visible/claimed | 2 |
| Has water/food support | 1 |
| Has parking/vehicle access | 1 |
| Has public business contact | 1 |
| Looks quiet/private, not crowded package camp | 1 |
| Mentions camping/farmstay/nature stay | 1 |
| Has strong photos or reviews | 1 |

Score:

- 8-10: founder call candidate.
- 5-7: nurture/research.
- 0-4: watch only.

## Daily Founder Message Template

Subject:

CampIn founding host idea for [property/location]

Message:

Hi [Name],

I am building CampIn, a trust-first camping platform for India. We are starting around Bangalore and South India with private land, farms, estates, homestays, and road stops where campers can stay only when permission, washrooms, water, parking, and local support are clear.

Your [property/business] stood out because [specific reason].

We are not asking hosts to join a big booking marketplace yet. We are shortlisting founding hosts for small manual pilots, with clear rules and founder support.

Would you be open to a 10-15 minute call to see if your place could work for responsible campers?

Best,
[Founder Name]
CampIn
hello@campin.co.in

## Follow-Up Rules

- Follow up once after 4-5 days.
- Do not guilt-trip.
- Do not imply urgency that is not real.
- If no response after one follow-up, mark as no response and stop.
- If the host declines, thank them and ask if they know anyone better suited.

## Daily Output Format

Each outreach automation should output:

- 10 researched candidates.
- Source URL/contact source.
- Segment.
- Location.
- Why relevant.
- Known amenities.
- Missing proof.
- Lead score.
- Draft message.
- Next action.
- Compliance note.

## Automation Boundary

Codex can research public business pages, prepare draft outreach, score leads, and update founder action lists.

Codex must not automatically send emails, DMs, WhatsApp messages, or form submissions on behalf of CampIn without explicit per-message approval.
