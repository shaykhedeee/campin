# CampIn 90-Day Execution Sprint

## Sprint Goal

Validate CampIn's first market before building a booking engine:

Bangalore/South India demand, permissioned private-land supply, and road-stop utility.

## Weekly Rhythm

Monday:

- Update scoreboard.
- Pick the week's validation priority.
- Select 10 research queries.
- Select 15 host/road-stop prospects.

Tuesday:

- Run research collection or manual source logging.
- Convert insights into survey questions, host leads, or content briefs.

Wednesday:

- Founder-led host outreach.
- Follow up on prior host replies.
- Schedule calls.

Thursday:

- Camper/community validation.
- Post one useful Reddit/community question where allowed.
- Send safety survey to new camper leads.

Friday:

- Draft and send The Campfire.
- Convert one insight into a content brief or FAQ.
- Review open replies.

Saturday:

- Host calls, site reviews, photo checks, or local mapping.

Sunday:

- Decide keep/pivot/stop for the week's experiment.

## Days 1-7: Foundation

Outcomes:

- Airtable base created from `data/research/schemas/research_os_fields.csv`.
- Tally/Typeform forms created from `data/forms/*.json`.
- Brevo/Mailchimp list and tags created.
- Welcome sequence loaded.
- First 50 host prospects identified.
- First 20 road-stop prospects identified.

Definition of done:

- Forms submit into Airtable.
- Welcome email sends.
- Founder receives submission notification.
- Test records are tagged correctly.

## Days 8-21: Research And Demand

Outcomes:

- 100 research insights logged.
- 100 camper waitlist leads.
- 25 high-intent own-gear or road-trip leads.
- Two Campfire issues sent.
- First safety survey results summarized.

Actions:

- Run 10 query batches.
- Post 3 community questions in appropriate public spaces.
- Ask every warm contact for one camper referral.
- Build the first FAQ from actual objections.

## Days 22-45: Supply

Outcomes:

- 100 host/road-stop prospects logged.
- 50 host outreach messages sent.
- 10 host replies.
- 5 host calls.
- 5 candidate sites with actual photos.

Actions:

- Focus on Coorg, Chikmagalur, Wayanad, Ramanagara, and Kanakapura.
- Prioritize farms/estates with washrooms and parking.
- Score each host using the Host Fit Score.
- Reject unclear permission early.

## Days 46-75: Road Stops And Pilot Candidates

Outcomes:

- 20 road-stop candidates.
- 5 candidates on Bangalore-Coorg-Wayanad.
- 3 overnight-permission conversations.
- 3 camper pilot candidates.
- 3 host pilot candidates.

Actions:

- Map route gaps before dark arrival points.
- Contact public business numbers only.
- Ask direct questions: overnight allowed, washroom, water, power, security.
- Do not publish exact stop claims until permission is confirmed.

## Days 76-90: Proof

Outcomes:

- 500 subscribers or clear growth path.
- 30 host applications/prospects with fit scores.
- 10 verified candidate sites.
- 3 manual booking attempts.
- 1 successful paid pilot stay or a scheduled paid pilot.

Actions:

- Match strongest camper and host.
- Write WhatsApp summary for both parties.
- Confirm price, rules, dates, and emergency contact.
- Collect post-stay feedback.
- Decide whether to build website forms/pages next.

## Keep/Pivot/Stop

Keep going if:

- Hosts respond and at least 10 are plausible.
- Campers ask for specific places or pilot access.
- Newsletter gets replies.
- Safety objections are clear and solvable.

Pivot if:

- People want guided group camping more than own-tent camping.
- Hosts prefer day picnic/events over overnight stays.
- Road stops are easier to validate than campsites.
- One region underperforms while another region shows stronger response.

Stop or rethink if:

- Campers will not share contact details.
- Hosts reject the concept after understanding it.
- Permission and safety cannot be handled responsibly.
- No one is willing to pay for a permissioned stay.
