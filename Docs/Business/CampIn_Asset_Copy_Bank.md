## Founder Story

India has the landscapes for camping: farms, valleys, forests, beaches, orchards, deserts, and endless road trips. What is missing is the trust layer.

Most people do not know where they can legally and safely pitch a tent. Hosts do not know how to welcome responsible campers without losing control of their land. Road travelers do not know where they can stop overnight with a washroom, water, and a local person aware of their stay.

CampIn exists to solve that gap. We are starting small, manually, and honestly: one region, one host, one road stop, one verified detail at a time.

The goal is not to build another travel listing website. The goal is to make camping in India feel possible.

Stay. Explore. CampIn.

## Safety Checklist Copy

Title:

CampIn Safe Camping Checklist

Checklist:

- Clear permission to stay overnight.
- Exact Google Maps pin.
- Reachable host or caretaker.
- Washroom access.
- Water access.
- Arrival before dark.
- Local rules confirmed.
- Weather checked.
- Emergency contact shared.
- Campsite cleaned before leaving.

Short note:

CampIn does not treat random public land as a campsite. Permission comes first.

## Responsible Camping Pledge

I will camp only where I have permission.

I will respect local people, land, wildlife, and quiet hours.

I will leave the site cleaner than I found it.

I will not light fires unless the host clearly allows it.

I will not enter protected forests, wildlife zones, or restricted areas without permission.

I will share my trip details with someone I trust.

I will help build a better camping culture in India.
