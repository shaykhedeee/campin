## SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM

### India's Outdoor Lovers Have Nowhere to Go

**For Campers:**
- 🔴 No organized platform to discover camping spots across India
- 🔴 No way to know if a campsite is safe, has water, washrooms, or electricity
- 🔴 No trusted reviews from real campers
- 🔴 No safe overnight stops for campervans and road travelers
- 🔴 Existing options are either overpriced glamping or unverified listings

**For Landowners:**
- 🔴 Beautiful land sits empty and generates no income
- 🔴 No easy way to reach the growing camping community
- 🔴 Fear of hosting strangers without a trust framework
- 🔴 No tools to manage bookings, payments, or guest communication

**The Market:**
- India's adventure tourism: $16.7 Billion (2024) → $86 Billion (2033)
- Camping equipment market: $892 Million → $2,027 Million by 2030
- 17.8% CAGR — one of the fastest-growing travel segments in India

## SLIDE 8: THE ROAD STOP REVOLUTION

### India's First Campervan Stop Directory

**The Problem:** More Indians than ever are traveling by campervan, SUV, and bike. But there is NO organized network of safe overnight stops.

**CampIn's Solution:** A dedicated "Road Stops" category with verified listings that include:
- ✅ Safe overnight parking
- ✅ Drinking water access
- ✅ Washroom facilities
- ✅ Electricity / charging (where available)
- ✅ Mobile signal verification
- ✅ Distance from highway
- ✅ Campfire allowance
- ✅ Community host rating

**First Routes Being Mapped:**
- Delhi → Manali (Himachal Pradesh)
- Pune → Goa (Maharashtra/Goa)
- Bangalore → Coorg (Karnataka)
- Mumbai → Kasol (Himachal Pradesh)
- Jaipur → Jaisalmer (Rajasthan)
