**Best for:** Decathlon, Wildcraft, Amazon Outdoor, camping gear brands

1. CampIn features your products in gear guides, camping checklists, and blog articles
2. Affiliate links drive traffic to your store
3. You pay 5-10% commission on referred sales
4. CampIn creates co-branded content: "CampIn × [Brand] Camping Checklist"

**What you get:**
- Direct access to India's most engaged camping audience
- Featured placement in CampIn's gear recommendation content
- Affiliate performance tracking and reporting

**Investment:** 5-10% commission on referred sales only (no upfront cost)

### MODEL C: State Tourism Board Partnership
**Best for:** Himachal Tourism, Kerala Tourism, Rajasthan Tourism, Uttarakhand Tourism

1. CampIn creates state-specific landing pages with verified campsites
2. Co-branded marketing: "Discover [State] Camping with CampIn × [Tourism Board]"
3. Tourism board promotes CampIn as the official camping platform
4. CampIn drives tourists to lesser-known camping destinations in the state

**What the state gets:**

**Investment:** ₹50,000-2,00,000 per state partnership
